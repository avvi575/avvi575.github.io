Dec 2021 – Mar 2025  |  Team of 10+

  • Designed Databricks Framework for Medallion Architecture (Bronze/Silver/Gold layers) with Delta Live Tables.
  • Developed ADF ELT pipelines extracting from .json, .xml, .csv, .xlsx, and API sources into ADLS.
  • Created metadata-driven Databricks Workflows enabling reusable, configurable pipeline orchestration.
  • Led the ETL team of 10+ engineers and managed deliverables across multiple project tracks with zero escalations.
  • Utilized Bulk Execution operations to load data into Azure Synapse Analytics at enterprise scale.
